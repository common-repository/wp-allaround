Connect your blog with your own (or other) Telegram Channel and spread your contents to the world ! 

== Description ==

WP-AllAround is the connector plugin for AllAroundSiena Telegram Bot service, that let you to spread via Telegram channels new posts of your blog.

Telegram channels are the best way to promote your blog and its content. Followers simply need to join your channel, using provided link, and 
receive directly on its smartphone any update and new articles published on your blog.

With AllAroundSiena, all you need to do is install the plugin, create a channel on Telegram Messenger platform and copy the Api Key on your 
configuration page on https://www.allaroundsiena.com

It's simple, it's free.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings->AllAround Options screen to configure the plugins and register your blog on AllAroundSiena.com
4. Wait for validation e-mail in your mailbox and follow the istructions
5. You're done ! Now you need to create you Telegram Channel and configure it as explained on http://www.allaroundsiena.com pages

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I need Telegram installed to use this ? =

Yes, because you need to create a channel/group where link your bot and invite your friend and followers. Don't worry: it's quite simple !

= How does it costs ? =

The "blog plan" it's free, forever, and include 1 blog and 1 post every 12 hours.
